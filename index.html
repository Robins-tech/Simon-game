How to Play Simon.

Getting Started

  • Press any key to begin the game.

Gameplay

  • Level 1: A single box will flash. Click on that box to advance to the next level.
  • Level 2 and beyond:
    • A sequence of boxes will flash.
    • Carefully watch the sequence.
    • Click the boxes in the exact order they were shown.
    • With each correct level, a new box will be added to the sequence.

Game Over

  • If you click the wrong box or miss a box, the game ends.
  • Press any key to start a new game.

Tips

  • Focus on the pattern and try to memorize the sequence.
  • The game gets progressively harder, so stay focused.
  • Enjoy the challenge and have fun!
